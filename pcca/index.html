Clustering the MSM

For now there is only one approach for clustering the microscopic MSM. Here we borrow the methods described by Buchete and Hummer to build macrostates based on the sign or distribution of the eigenvector values.

  • Coarse Master Equations for Peptide Folding Dynamics J. Phys. Chem. B 112 (19) 6057â€“6069 (2008).

As they stated in their paper, this approach is a version of Perron clustering. On top of this, and following work by Chodera et al we include an optimization step in the clustering. This way, there is some chance that imperfections in the initial, eigenvector-based clustering, will be overcome. The optimization is a simple Monte Carlo simulated annealing procedure with a simple temperature scheme.
